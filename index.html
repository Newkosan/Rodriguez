Recipe: Sinigang na Baboy

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork (spare ribs or belly), cut into chunks
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 2 medium taro (gabi), peeled and cubed
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2–3 pcs green chili (siling haba)
  • 1 pack tamarind soup base (sinigang mix)
  • Fish sauce (patis) or salt, to taste
  • 6 cups water

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil and add the pork. Simmer until the meat becomes tender.
  2. Add onions and tomatoes. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add taro and continue simmering until soft.
  4. Add radish, eggplant, and green chili. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in the sinigang mix and season with fish sauce or salt.
  6. Finally, add kangkong leaves and simmer for 2 more minutes.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
